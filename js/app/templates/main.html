The annual European Ruby Conference will take place

About

EuRuKo is the annual European Ruby Conference on Ruby programming language
and satellite technologies. Its awesome attendees chat, hack, and share in a friendly atmosphere.

Since 2003, EuRuKo has guested all over the Europe, organized by Ruby developers for Ruby developers.
Salute Kyiv, the next amazing conference’s host!

The conference is organized on by local developers who welcome you
on 20th—21st of June, 2014 to hang out with the community.

Dive into the heart of tech gigs, and Ruby fans sharing, discussing, and simply having great time!

Come and love the fun!

Venue

hall

We will gather this time in the International Center of Culture and Arts in Kyiv, Ukraine. That is a fancy spot for famous writers, composers, and other public figures, a sort of an architectural reflection of the spiritual renaissance of Ukraine. The building was constructed at the beginning of the 20th century.

stage

It’s designed in the classical style to become the greatest concert hall in Kyiv being able to accommodate for about 2000 people. Spacious rooms of the Centre's foyer will host in June of 2014 for about 600 Ruby lovers! And the city itself will embrace its visitors with Slavic generosity and kindness of locals.
